W3School offers a lot of tutorials for various languages that will be helpful when becoming a Computer Science major.

Code Academy offers lessons for languages, it even tracks your progress of which languages and lessons you've completed.

Code Wars offers a fun and more community driven way of learning and practicing languages of your choosing.

TryHackMe has interactive lessons where you will get hands-on experience with network simulations and intentionally vulnerable technology based on real world examples.

HackTheBox provides virtual environments where you will be introduced to cybersecurity concepts in a gamified way. You will learn hacking tools and techniques and then use them to exploit common vulnerabilities and misconfigurations.

The National Cyber League (NCL) is a virtual cybersecurity competition with an emphasis on teaching high school and college students about the field of cybersecurity. The NCL has coaches that will lead practice sessions and answer any questions you may have. The NCL also offers training workshops and seminars to all participants.

How can I be prepared to be a computer Science Major?

You can be well versed and prepared for being a computer science major by practicing the field you would like to be in and reviewing and completing the tutorials that are listed on this page
